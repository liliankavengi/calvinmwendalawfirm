About Calvin Mwenda Co & Advocates

Two decades of legal excellence and unwavering commitment to justice

Our History & Mission

Founded in 2003 by Calvin Mwenda, our firm has grown from a small practice to one of Kenya's most respected legal institutions. We built our reputation on three core principles: excellence, integrity, and client service.

Our mission is to provide exceptional legal services while maintaining the highest ethical standards. We believe that every client deserves personalized attention and strategic solutions that address their unique challenges.

Today, our team of 15 experienced advocates serves clients across Kenya and internationally, handling everything from complex corporate transactions to sensitive family matters.
